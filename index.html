Online Book Sale Update

OUUC's annual online book sale will now be held in mid-October, to showcase some of the most interesting book, movie, and music donations - and make them super easy for you to find among the many thousands of donations we receive for our Books Brownies & Beans used book sale extravaganza.

Our 2024 SHOWCASE online Book Store will be open HERE from:

7am Tuesday October 15 thru 10pm Sunday October 20

Your combined, paid SHOWCASE orders will be waiting for you to pick up any time during the in-person portion of our 2024 Books Brownies & Beans used book sale extravaganza on Saturday, October 26 at the Olympia Unitarian Universalist Congregation (open from 9am to 4pm at 2306 East End St NW, Olympia, WA 98502). You can shop many more thousands of wonderful books while you listen to live music and enjoy homemade brownies and delicious coffee or tea.

Meanwhile, please keep those book donations coming! Gently used books are welcome during any daylight hours at the covered center section of Susan Dodson's front deck. Susan's address is in the OUUC directory, or you can email Susan for more information. Please do not deliver your donations directly to the OUUC building at this time of the year.
