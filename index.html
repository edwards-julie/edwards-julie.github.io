Bring on the Books! Donations of gently used books, audiobooks, CDs and DVDs for OUUC's community book sales are welcome during any daylight hours at the covered center section of Susan Dodson's front deck in Olympia, WA. Susan's address is in the OUUC directory, or you can email Susan at books.ouuc@gmail.com for more information. Please do not deliver your donations to the OUUC building.
