Our 2023 SUURF online used bookstore for Olympia-Area shoppers will open HERE at 8am Tuesday, June 13 and close at 10pm Sunday, June 18.

Sale proceeds will benefit three local non-profit organizations working towards a more loving, just, and healthy world. The beneficiaries for 2023 are Interfaith Works, ASHHO Cultural Community Center, and OlyEcosystems.

While the SUURF bookstore is open, you can shop and pay online, then pick up your purchases the following week at:

Olympia Unitarian Universalist Congregation
2315 Division St NW, Olympia, WA 98502
