Book Nook

Is coming soon!

Presented by:

2306 East End St NW        Olympia, Washington 98502

Book Nook will be the Olympia Unitarian Universalist Congregations' online used bookstore for Olympia-area shoppers, open '24/7' on an ongoing basis. You'll be able to shop and pay securely online and then arrange to pick up your orders from an OUUC book sale team volunteer. And, just as with OUUC's annual Fall in-person Books Brownies & Beans used book sale event, proceeds will be lovingly passed on to our community partners working towards a more loving, just, and healthy world.

We plan to open Book Nook within a few weeks. You can sign up below to receive updates.
